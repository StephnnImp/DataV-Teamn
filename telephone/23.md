## Parameters

Length will vary with *String*.

Property|Index|Length|Type
:-------|:----|:-----|:---
Layout|`0`|`1`|Bitfield
thing|`1`|`1`|Bitfield
Options|`2`|`1`|Bitfield
String|`3`|`-1`|String

---

### Layout
    
    DEFAULT = 0x00
    PIN     = 0x05
    DIAL    = 0x42  # Last Numbers

---

### thing
    
    THING            = 0x00
---

### Options

    BOB       = 0b0001_1111
