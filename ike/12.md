# `0x12` Sensors Request

Request sensor status `0x13` from cluster.

### Related

`0x13` [Sensors](13.md)

## Examples

    30 03 80 12 A1  # CCM (Seat belt warning?)
    5B 03 80 12 CA  # IHKA
    60 03 80 12 F1  # PDC (gear?)
    AC 03 80 12 3D  # EHC
    ED 03 80 12 7C  # TV

None!
