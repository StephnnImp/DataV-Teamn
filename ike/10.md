# `0x10` Ignition Request

Request the ignition status from the cluster.

### Related

- `0x11` [Ignition](11.md)

### Examples

    18 03 80 10 8B  # CDC
    30 03 80 10 A3  # CCM
    3B 03 80 10 A8  # GT
    AC 03 80 10 3F  # EHC
    ED 03 80 10 7E  # TV
    F0 03 80 10 63  # BMBT

## Parameters

None!
