### Example Frames

    80 04 FF 57 01 2D
    80 04 FF 57 41 6D
    80 04 FF 57 02 2E

## Parameters

Fixed length. 1-byte bitfield.

    STATE   = 0b0100_0000
    BUTTON  = 0b0000_0011

### State `0b0100_0000`

Unlike other buttons, there is no hold event.

    STATE_PRESS     = 0b0
    STATE_RELEASE   = 0b1

### Button `0b0000_0011`

    BUTTON_CHECK    = 0b01  # CHECK button on HIGH clusters
    BUTTON_BC       = 0b10  # BC button on stalk

I'm only aware of two buttons thus far.
